[[Small Language Model]] ([[SLM]])

Compared to a Large Language Model, a SLM typically has fewer parameters, allowing for faster processing and lower memory usage. However, it may not possess the same level of knowledge or context awareness as a Large Language Model due to its smaller size. Small Language Models are often used for specific tasks or applications where resource efficiency is crucial, or as a starting point for research before moving to larger models.

Typically defined as ~1b parameters to ~70b parameters
